Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Product Setup & Installation

  • How do I set up my new SSD?

    • Connect the SSD to your computer via USB or SATA. Download our setup guide from the product page or contact support for step-by-step video tutorials.

  • Can I use DataVault NAS with multiple devices?

    • Yes! Our NAS systems support up to 10 simultaneous connections. Ensure your network is secure and follow the user manual for configuration.

Troubleshooting

  • My external hard drive isn't recognized by my computer.

    • Try a different USB port or cable. If issues persist, run our diagnostic tool (available for download on our site) or contact support for hardware checks.

  • Data recovery from a failed drive?

    • We offer data recovery services for eligible products under warranty. Email support@oceanGate.com with details for a quote.

Warranty/Return

  • What's the warranty period?

    • Most products come with a 3-year limited warranty. Check your product packaging or our website for specifics, or contact our support team for detailed warranty assistance.

  • How to return a product?

    • Initiate a return via our online portal within 30 days. We'll provide a prepaid label for eligible returns.

Security and Privacy

  • Is my data safe on DataVault cloud storage?

    • Absolutely. We use end-to-end encryption, multi-factor authentication, and comply with GDPR and CCPA standards. Learn more in our privacy policy.
